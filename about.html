Solar Energy

Photovoltaic

Converts sunlight into electricity using photovoltaic cells. Output depends on panel area, efficiency, solar resource, and system losses.

E = Area × η × Irradiance × PR
  • Area — panel surface in m²
  • η — panel efficiency (0 – 1)
  • Irradiance — kWh/m²/day
  • PR — performance ratio (0.70 – 0.85)
🌬️

Wind Energy

Turbine

Wind turbines extract kinetic energy from moving air. Power scales with the cube of wind speed. The Betz limit (0.593) is the theoretical maximum Cp.

P = ½ × ρ × A × v³ × Cp × η
  • ρ — air density (1.225 kg/m³ std)
  • A — rotor swept area (π × r²)
  • v — wind speed (m/s)
  • Cp — power coefficient (max 0.593)
  • η — generator efficiency
